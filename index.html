Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta Okkotsu is the main character in the prequel series "Jujutsu Kaisen 0." He is a student at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College and hosts a powerful Cursed Spirit, Rika Orimoto, which grants him immense strength but also burdens him with great responsibility. His journey involves mastering his abilities and understanding the true nature of his curse.

Initially, Yuta appears timid and overwhelmed by his curse, but his character grows significantly as he learns to control his powers and confront his past. His evolution from a fearful student to a formidable sorcerer highlights his inner strength and resilience.

Yuta's story is one of personal growth, confronting his fears, and finding purpose in a world filled with curses and danger. His relationship with Rika adds emotional depth to his character and motivates his quest for redemption.

Gojo Satoru
Jujutsu Kaisen

Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is one of the most powerful sorcerers in the "Jujutsu Kaisen" series. As a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College, he plays a crucial role in guiding and training young sorcerers like Yuji Itadori and Yuta Okkotsu. His immense skill and charismatic personality make him a central figure in the fight against curses.

Known for his laid-back attitude and overwhelming confidence, Gojo possesses unparalleled strength and skill in jujutsu techniques. His abilities, combined with his unique perspective on the world of curses, make him a complex and influential character.

Despite his playful demeanor, Gojo is deeply committed to protecting humanity from curses and shaping the next generation of sorcerers. His presence adds both excitement and depth to the series, making him a fan favorite.

Geto Suguru
Jujutsu Kaisen

Geto Suguru

Geto Suguru is a former student of the Jujutsu High School and a central antagonist in "Jujutsu Kaisen." Once a promising sorcerer, Geto becomes disillusioned with the world of jujutsu and turns against his former allies. His transformation from a respected sorcerer to a powerful antagonist is driven by his belief that the world is fundamentally flawed.

Geto's abilities and ideology make him a formidable enemy. His vision for a world where curses rule is a significant threat to humanity, and his actions challenge the very foundations of the jujutsu society. His complex character adds depth and intrigue to the storyline.

As a powerful sorcerer with a strong conviction, Geto's role in the series is pivotal in exploring themes of morality, power, and the consequences of one's choices. His character serves as a foil to other protagonists and highlights the struggle between different ideologies within the world of jujutsu.

Yuki Tsukumo
Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuki Tsukumo

Yuki Tsukumo is a special grade sorcerer in "Jujutsu Kaisen" known for her impressive skills and powerful abilities. As a mentor and ally to the series' main characters, Yuki plays a crucial role in the fight against curses and the development of young sorcerers.

Her character is characterized by a strong sense of duty and a deep understanding of jujutsu techniques. Yuki's presence in the series is both inspiring and formidable, showcasing her expertise and commitment to the jujutsu society's goals.

Despite her formidable powers, Yuki also faces personal challenges and conflicts that add complexity to her character. Her interactions with other sorcerers and her role in the ongoing battles against curses highlight her significance in the storyline.
